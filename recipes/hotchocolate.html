Hot Chocolate

Delicous gourmet hot chocolate is a very easy recipe that will replace instant-pouch for the rest of your life, in no time you will find yourself showing friends and family this great recipe!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups milk
  • Half a cup of chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Put milk in a pot and bring to a simmer
  2. Put chocolate chips into simmering milk and stir until melted and no chunks
  3. Pour into mug and let cool. Serve with spoon and keep stiring to prevent milk and chocolate from seperating
