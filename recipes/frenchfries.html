French Fries

Super easy and healthy snack that will teach cutting and seasoning!

Ingredients

  • 1 potatoe
  • 1 tbsp salt
  • 1 tbsp pepper
  • 1 tbsp olive oil

Steps

  1. Pre-heat oven to 425
  2. Cut potatoe length-wise into thin cuttings
  3. Cut cuttings again length-wise into fries
  4. Put fries, olive oil, salt and pepper into a bowl
  5. Toss fries and seasoning in bowl
  6. Put parchment paper on a baking sheet, put fries on baking sheet in single layer with space in between
  7. Bake for 20 mins, take out and flip then bake for 10 more mins
  8. Let cool and serve with ketchup or mayo as dip!
